Why Veritell? How it Works

Caregiving is hard enough.

We understand the stress of "missing something" in the flood of medical information. You are the advocate, the nurse, and the coordinator. You deserve a tool that works as hard as you do.

Fragmented Records

Stop logging into five different portals. We connect to Epic, MyChart, and more to pull everything into one timeline.

Medical Jargon

Don't guess what "CBC with Differential" means. Our AI explains complex terms in plain English so you can ask better questions.

Manage Updates

Get in control of the information coming at you. We provide regular, automatic summaries to help you stay on top of lab results, notes, and more.

Private by Design.

Your data is yours. We believe health information is sacred, and we treat it that way.

Local-First

Your data lives on your device, encrypted and safe. We don't store your medical records in the cloud.

Bank-Grade Encryption

We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption to ensure that only you hold the keys to your data.

No Data Selling

We are not an ad company. We never sell, trade, or share your personal health information.
